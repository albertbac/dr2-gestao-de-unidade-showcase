# Healthcare Unit Operations and Indicator Management

## 1. App name
Gestao de unidade

## 2. One-line summary
A public-safe technical case for healthcare unit operations, indicators, prioritization, and coordinated management workflows.

## 3. Primary user
Healthcare unit manager

## 4. Secondary user
Operations or quality team

## 5. Problem solved
Unit managers often handle fragmented signals, scattered indicators, and disconnected action plans with weak traceability.

## 6. Short workflow
Operational inputs feed dashboards, indicators are reviewed, actions are prioritized, teams execute follow-ups, and progress is revisited in a single workspace.

## 7. Public-safe technical scope
Public-safe scope covers dashboards, indicator management, review cycles, action orchestration, and audit-friendly management surfaces.

## 8. High-level integrations
High-level only: identity, persistence, charting, background jobs, and reporting layers.

## 9. What stays private and why
Thresholds, client-specific indicators, internal escalation logic, and operational configuration remain private because they are product and customer-specific.

## 10. Confidence level
HIGH

## 11. Exposure risk
MEDIUM
